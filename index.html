ABOUT

Ethan Spetnagel is an interdisciplinary designer based in Queens, New York.

He is a Manager of Design & Fulfillment at Church California – a skincare and grooming brand. Additionally, he is a UX Designer for Talamel Health Technologies – a healthcare start-up that's revolutionizing how people find post-acute care.

In 2023, he designed and founded Slug, an artisanal soap brand where he handled everything from product development and packaging to digital branding.

He graduated from the University of Colorado with a dual major in Information Analytics and Finance.

Available for freelance and collaborative work.
