Church California

About Work Other work

Church California is a haircare and skincare brand based in San Francisco that prides itself on creating high quality, all-natural, locally-sourced products. As Design & Fulfillment Manager, I oversee creative content development while improving operational workflows. My work has contributed to growth in both social media engagement (44% uptick since starting) and order volume through strategic visual storytelling. Since starting, I've supported the launch of multiple product lines including the Supernatural Serum, Rosehip Oil, Deodorant Bar, Botanical Barbershop Merch, and the complete Church Shave collection.

At Church, I create visual content that blends our craft-focused approach with a clean, modern feel. My work spans everything from quick 10-second Instagram stories to in-depth YouTube videos. Each piece reflects Church California's dedication to natural ingredients and timeless design, using a palette of off-whites, off-blacks, and our signature international orange. I keep typography classic with typefaces like Futura and Knockout to create visuals that last.

Because we're a small team, I wear several hats—from shooting on-location footage and building 3D models in Blender to creating motion graphics in After Effects and handling color grading and audio cleanup in Premiere.

SKINCARE CAMPAIGN

This campaign introduced our full skincare line: the Botanical Cleanser, Rosehip Oil, Supernatural Serum, and Neroli Water. The goal was to spread the word about our skincare while showcasing the natural, botanical ingredients inside. We filmed in several Northern California locations, capturing nature footage in places like the Marin Headlands to reinforce our nature-first philosophy. This helped position our products as high-quality and eco-friendly alternatives to mass-produced options with unrecognizable ingredients.

POMADE CAMPAIGN

For this campaign, I focused on our Coastal Crème Pomade, Sun Wax Pomade, and Volcanic Clay Pomade. Again leaning into Northern California's beauty, I created engaging Instagram ads featuring customer testimonials and recognition from GQ, Robb Report, and Men's Health. Highlighting these awards built credibility and trust with our audience.

BRANDING VIDEOS

Beyond specific products, I've worked on content that strengthens Church California's overall brand identity. Since Church runs an actual barbershop in San Francisco, I produced videos featuring real customers and haircuts, reinforcing that our products are "barber tested and barber approved." This messaging builds authentic trust by showing that professionals who work with hair every day use our products.

I also created longer videos like our Botanical Barbershop feature, which showcase our full product range with more thoughtful "chef's table"-style imagery. These videos highlight our dedication to quality and craft, using organic transitions and botanical visuals that echo our ingredients while keeping a clean, premium aesthetic.

Beyond video work, I've worked on various other projects for Church California's visual identity. This includes input on packaging design, collaboration with lead designer Daniel Castro, creating custom sticker designs, and developing graphics for email campaigns and print materials. These elements work together to maintain a cohesive brand experience across all touchpoints.
