OTHER PROJECTS

CHURCH CALIFORNIA

ABOUT VIDEO OTHER

Church California is a San Francisco brand making haircare and skincare products from all-natural plant ingredients. They sell pomades, skincare products, and merch. As Design & Fulfillment Manager, I oversee creative content development while optimizing operational workflows for Church. My work has contributed to growth in both social media engagement (44% uptick since starting) and order volume through strategic visual storytelling.

DESIGN

- Marketing print + digital assets
- Motion graphics
- Social media content
- Product packaging
- On-location shoots (concept to final edit)
- Product launch campaigns (Supernatural Serum, Deodorant, Shaving Cream, Razor, Body Bar)

OPERATIONS

- Improved website UX (improved checkout flow, product filtering)
-Streamlined packaging + shipping processes
- Inventory + order workflow management
- Wholesale outreach

Each campaign below represents months of strategic development, from initial concept to final deployment. The Skincare Campaign features our complete line of botanical cleansers and serums, captured against the raw beauty of the Marin Headlands to reinforce our nature-first philosophy. The Pomade series leverages customer testimonials and 3D visualization to build trust while showcasing product texture and hold. Our Brand Identity films document the authentic barbershop culture where these products are born, tested, and perfected by professional barbers before reaching consumers.

Working closely with founder Michael Haar and lead designer Daniel Castro, I've crafted visual narratives that resonate across platforms—from 6-second Instagram stories to long-form YouTube features. Each piece balances Church California's artisanal roots with contemporary design sensibilities, creating content that not only captures attention but drives meaningful engagement and conversion. The motion graphics employ organic transitions and botanical motifs that echo our ingredients, while maintaining the clean, professional aesthetic expected in the premium grooming market.

This portfolio showcases my comprehensive approach to brand storytelling through motion design, where every frame serves both aesthetic and strategic purposes. The work demonstrates proficiency across the full creative pipeline: from shooting on-location footage and creating photorealistic 3D models in Blender, to developing complex After Effects compositions and color grading in Premiere Pro. Each campaign has contributed to Church California's 44% growth in social engagement and expanded wholesale presence.

Beyond video work, I've worked on various other projects for Church California's visual identity. This includes input on packaging design, collaboration with lead designer Daniel Castro, creating custom sticker designs, and developing graphics for email campaigns and print materials. These elements work together to maintain a cohesive brand experience across all touchpoints.
