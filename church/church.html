CHURCH CALIFORNIA

ABOUT VIDEO OTHER

Church California is a San Francisco brand making haircare and skincare products from all-natural plant ingredients. They sell pomades, hair products, and body care essentials that are 100% plant-based. As Design & Fulfillment Manager, I oversee creative content development while optimizing operational workflows for Church. My work has contributed to growth in both social media engagement (44% uptick since starting) and order volume through strategic visual storytelling.

DESIGN WORK:

- Print materials and digital assets for our marketing
- Motion graphics
- Social media content
- Product packaging
- On-location shoots from concept to final edit
- Launch campaigns for our new products (Supernatural Serum, Deodorant, Shaving Cream, Razor, Body Bar)

OPERATIONS WORK:

- Making our website easier to shop (improved checkout flow, product filtering)
- Streamlining how we package and ship orders
- Managing inventory and order processing workflows
- Handling quality checks before products go out
- Reaching out to potential wholesale accounts

My video work for Church California spans multiple campaigns and platforms, each designed to capture the brand's botanical essence while driving conversion. Through a combination of motion graphics, 3D animation, and lifestyle footage, I've developed a visual language that connects our all-natural ethos with contemporary design aesthetics.

Each campaign is meticulously crafted for multi-platform deployment—from 6-second bumper ads to full YouTube features. The work showcases my proficiency in After Effects, Blender, and Premiere Pro, creating content that not only elevates the brand but delivers measurable results in engagement and sales.

This portfolio represents a comprehensive approach to brand storytelling, where every frame serves both aesthetic and strategic purposes, reinforcing Church California's position as a leader in botanical grooming products.

Beyond video work, I've worked on various other projects for Church California's visual identity. This includes input on packaging design, collaboration with lead designer Daniel Castro, creating custom sticker designs, and developing graphics for email campaigns and print materials. These elements work together to maintain a cohesive brand experience across all touchpoints.

BARBERSHOP DOCUMENTARY SERIES
SOFTWARE: AFTER EFFECTS, PREMIERE PRO
CONCEPT: PRODUCT AUTHENTICITY NARRATIVE

This brand film showcases the symbiotic relationship between Church California products and the barbershop where they're conceived and tested. Through dynamic zoom transitions created in After Effects, the piece weaves together the dual narratives of craftsmanship and commerce. The documentary approach legitimizes our "made by barbers, for everyone" positioning while highlighting the rigorous product development process that occurs within the shop's walls.

3D PRODUCT VISUALIZATION
INFO

3D PRODUCT MODELING
SOFTWARE: BLENDER, AFTER EFFECTS
TECHNIQUE: PHOTOREALISTIC RENDERING

I developed a comprehensive library of photorealistic 3D models for the entire Church California product line using Blender. These digital assets enable dynamic motion graphics that would be impossible with traditional product photography. By integrating these models into Instagram advertisements and motion campaigns, I've created a distinctive visual language that sets our content apart in crowded social feeds. The flexibility of 3D assets allows for rapid iteration and seasonal campaign variations without additional photography costs.
