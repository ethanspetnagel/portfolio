TALAMEL HEALTH TECH

About UX

Status: In Progress

Talamel Health Tech is a healthcare startup building a platform that connects patients with post-acute care facilities, making the process simpler for patients, RNs, and facility staff. The goal is to create one intuitive platform where all the information needed to make informed post-acute care decisions is easily accessible.

I'm currently working as a UX Designer on our first product, Dana:Connect, which is set to be released to a limited audience in July 2025 for further testing. My role involves leading UX research by meeting regularly with RNs and case managers to understand their workflows and gather feedback on how they would actually use the product. These insights directly inform my design decisions to ensure the interface is clear, efficient, and touch-friendly for daily use.

Alongside product design, I am also developing a new brand identity for Talamel, including a redesigned logo and comprehensive brand guidelines to establish a cohesive, confident visual presence as we move toward launch.

This work is ongoing as we continue to refine Dana:Connect into a seamless tool that supports care teams and improves outcomes for patients. If you'd like to learn more about this project, please feel free to reach out to me.
